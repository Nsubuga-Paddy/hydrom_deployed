About Us

Under the ownership of UEGCL, Hydro-M is a waterway and level monitoring system that helps to extract insights of how climatic conditions affect hydro power generation.

Hydro-M uses machine learning algorithms to make predictions of water levels in dam reserviors along the CASCADE. It is also a system that shows cascaded dams activities i.e. dispatch and discharge in realtime.
